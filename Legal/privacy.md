# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Tama ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Discord bot.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use Tama, we automatically collect and store:

- **Discord User ID**: Your unique Discord identifier
- **Discord Server (Guild) ID**: The ID of servers where you use Tama
- **Timestamps**: When you interact with the bot and when data is created/modified

### User-Generated Information

We only store information you create through bot interactions:

- **Pet Data**: Pet names, types, stats (hunger, energy, love levels), and status
- **Inventory**: Items you own, quantities, and expiration dates
- **Currency**: Bells and stars you've earned
- **Game Statistics**: Performance in minigames, daily challenge participation
- **Settings**: Your personal preferences (DM notifications, skip confirmations)
- **Server Settings**: Server-specific configurations (for server administrators)
- **Pet Names**: Names you choose for your virtual pets

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

- **Provide Bot Functionality**: Store and manage your pets, inventory, currency, and progress
- **Game Mechanics**: Track stats, decay rates, and game performance
- **Personalization**: Apply your settings and preferences
- **Server Features**: Enable server-specific configurations
- **Service Improvement**: Analyze aggregated, anonymized data to improve features
- **Moderation**: Enforce our Terms of Service and maintain service integrity
- **Communication**: Send DM notifications if you've enabled them

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### Where Data is Stored

- All data is stored in a secure database
- Database is hosted on our private server infrastructure
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data

### Data Retention

- Active user data is retained indefinitely to maintain service continuity
- Data for inactive users may be retained unless deletion is requested
- Blacklisted users' data may be retained for security and moderation purposes
- Some data may be retained for legal compliance or security analysis

### Security Measures

While we implement reasonable security measures:
- No system is 100% secure
- We cannot guarantee absolute security
- You use the service at your own risk
- We are not liable for unauthorized access or data breaches

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do Not Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### When We May Share Data

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **With Discord**: Necessary information to provide bot functionality within Discord's platform
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law, court order, or legal process
- **Service Protection**: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize us to share information

## 5. Data Control and Your Rights

### Accessing Your Data

You can view most of your data through bot commands:
- `/profile` - View your pet, stats, and currency
- Inventory and badges buttons in your profile
- `/settings user` - View and modify your preferences

You can also request a copy of your data by contacting us through our [support server](https://discord.gg/bgCPw8rHva).

### Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your data:

**How to Request Deletion:**
- Contact us through our [support server](https://discord.gg/bgCPw8rHva)
- Specify that you want your data deleted
- We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe

**What Happens When You Delete Data:**
- All your pets, items, currency, and progress will be permanently deleted
- This action cannot be undone
- You can start fresh by using the bot again after deletion
- Some data may be retained for security, legal, or moderation purposes (e.g., blacklist records)

**Automatic Deletion:**
We may automatically delete data for:
- Accounts that have been inactive for extended periods
- Users who have deleted their Discord accounts
- At our discretion for data management purposes

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of Tama after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified terms. We are not required to notify users of changes.

## 7. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that compromises your information:
- We will assess the severity and scope of the breach
- We may notify affected users through Discord or our support server
- We are not legally obligated to provide notification in all cases
- We will take reasonable steps to mitigate the breach

## 8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, concerns, or data deletion requests:
- Join our [support server](https://discord.gg/bgCPw8rHva)
- Contact us through official channels
- Include relevant details (Discord user ID, nature of request)

**Response Time:**
- We aim to respond to privacy requests within a reasonable timeframe
- Complex requests may take longer to process
- We reserve the right to verify your identity before processing requests
