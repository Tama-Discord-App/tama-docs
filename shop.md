Learn how to earn currency and buy items for your pet.

## The Shop

Use `/shop` to browse available items. Items are organized into categories:

{% hint style="info" %}
Keep in mind each server may have a different shop!
{% endhint %}

### Item Categories

- **Food** - Restores hunger
- **Toys** - Increases love/happiness
- **Energy** - Restores energy
- **Special** - Unique items like name tags and revival items

### Item Details

Each item shows:
- **Name and description**
- **Price** (bells or stars)
- **Effect** - What it does for your pet
- **Stock** - Some items have limited availability

## Buying Items

To purchase an item:
1. Use `/shop` to open the shop
2. Browse items and select one
3. Confirm your purchase
4. The item goes to your inventory

{% hint style="warning" %}
Make sure you have enough currency before buying! Purchases are final.
{% endhint %}

## Using Items

Items you buy are stored in your inventory:

1. Open your profile with `/profile`
2. Click the "Inventory" button
3. Select an item to use
4. Confirm to apply it to your pet

### Item Effects

Items take effect immediately:
- **Food items** - Increase hunger by listed amount
- **Energy items** - Restore energy
- **Toys** - Boost love/happiness
- **Name tags** - Prompt you to rename your pet
- **Revival items** - Bring your pet back from death

## Earning Bells

You can earn bells through:

### Minigames
Play games using `/play random` or `/play daily`:
- Fishing - Catch fish for bell rewards
- Treasure Hunt - Find treasure chests
- Memory Match - Match pairs
- Dice Patterns - Roll matching patterns
- Target Practice - Hit targets

The better you perform, the more bells you earn!

{% hint style="success" %}
Daily challenges offer bonus multipliers and bigger rewards!
{% endhint %}

## Earning Stars

Stars are rarer and earned through:

### Achievements
Complete server-wide goals to earn star rewards. Check `/achievements` to see available goals and progress.

### Special Events
Seasonal events may offer star rewards for participation.

{% hint style="success" %}
Focus on completing achievements to earn stars for rare pets!
{% endhint %}

## Item Stacking

Most items stack in your inventory, so buying multiples won't clutter your space. The inventory shows the quantity you own.

## Shop Updates

The shop inventory may change over time:
- Seasonal items during special events
- Limited-time exclusive items
- New items added with updates

Check the shop regularly to see what's new!
