# Minigames

Play fun games to earn bells and keep your pet entertained.

## How to Play Games

There are two ways to play minigames:

### Play Random Game
Launch a randomly selected minigame with the `/play random` command. Great for variety!

### Play Daily Challenge
Play today's featured game via the `/play daily` command with special modifiers and bonus rewards. You can only play the daily challenge once per day.

{% hint style="success" %}
Daily challenges offer higher rewards and unique modifiers like extended rounds or special scoring!
{% endhint %}

## Available Games

Tama includes five different minigames. When you use `/play random` or `/play daily`, one of these games will launch:

### Fishing

Cast your line and catch fish for bells!

**How it Works:**
1. The fishing game launches automatically
2. Wait for a fish to bite
3. Click "Catch!" when prompted
4. Catch successfully to earn bells

**Tips:**
- Different fish are worth different amounts
- Timing is important - don't click too early!
- Rare fish give bigger rewards

### Treasure Hunt

Search for hidden treasure and dig it up!

**How it Works:**
1. The treasure hunt game launches automatically
2. Select a spot to dig
3. Find treasure chests for bells
4. Avoid empty spots

**Tips:**
- You get multiple attempts per game
- Treasure locations are random each time
- Bigger treasures mean more bells

### Memory Match

Match pairs of cards to win bells.

**How it Works:**
1. The memory game launches automatically
2. Click cards to flip them
3. Match pairs to remove them
4. Clear all cards to win

**Tips:**
- Remember card positions
- Fewer moves = better rewards
- Practice improves your memory!

### Dice Patterns

Roll dice and match target patterns.

**How it Works:**
1. The dice game launches automatically
2. See the target pattern
3. Roll your dice
4. Match the pattern to earn bells

**Tips:**
- You get multiple rolls per game
- Harder patterns give bigger rewards
- Choose your re-rolls wisely

### Target Practice

Hit moving targets to score points.

**How it Works:**
1. The target practice game launches automatically
2. Click on targets as they appear
3. Hit as many as possible before time runs out
4. Higher scores mean more bells

**Tips:**
- Focus on accuracy over speed
- Special targets may be worth more points
- Practice makes perfect

## Game Rewards

All games reward you with bells based on performance:
- **Better performance** = more bells
- **Perfect plays** may give bonus rewards
- **Completing games** contributes to achievements

## Achievements

Many games have related achievements:
- Play a certain number of times
- Reach high scores
- Win with perfect accuracy

Check `/achievements` to see game-related goals.
